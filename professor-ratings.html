Professor Ratings

Real reviews from brothers · Grades, difficulty, and what to expect

📊 How to read these: Difficulty and Quality are rated out of 5. Difficulty 1/5 = very easy · 5/5 = very hard. Grade shown is what the reviewer received.

ACCT 2001

Peters, Kimberly
Grade: A-
Difficulty: 4/5
Quality: 3/5
Textbook: Wiley Plus (required)
Attendance required. The tests test you on application — a lot more than just memorizing. To do well, really understand your homeworks and memorize study guides. Going to SI is helpful but not 100% necessary. The tests only get harder and the final is the hardest. Definitely hard to make an A+ but very easy to end with a B-. As long as you do your homeworks, study hard, and genuinely try, you will be fine. Mrs. Peters is the best teacher for this class — she fills out the study guide for you and is very helpful if you're struggling.

FIN 3351

Slawson, C
Grade: A
Difficulty: 3/5
Quality: 5/5
Textbook: Yes
TAKE HIS CLASSES!! Slawson is probably the best professor you'll have. He makes sure you know what's important for exams and truly wants you to learn the material. Attendance is required. Didn't take his classes until last semester and regret it so much.

GBUS 3061

Thompson, Melissa
Grade: A
Difficulty: 3/5
Quality: 5/5
Textbook: No (Emergenetics purchase required)
One class a week. Attendance required. Communication-Intensive (C-I) course. Professor Thompson is amazing and super helpful — she really wants students to succeed. Includes writing your resume, elevator pitch, cover letters, LinkedIn, emailing professionally, interview prep, and a group project. The class ends a week before finals. If you're going into any professional setting (business, sales, marketing), take this class — I recommend senior year. Couldn't recommend enough!

SW 2000

Rizzuto, Jessica
Grade: A+
Difficulty: 1/5
Quality: 5/5
Textbook: Yes
If you're thinking about the social work field or just need an easy elective, take this class! Rizzuto is super sweet and really cares about her students. She's a full-time social worker and this is the only class she teaches. Attendance is mandatory but she cancels class at least once a week. All coursework is on Moodle at your own pace. All quizzes can be found on Quizlet or in the textbook. It's a service learning course so you do have to volunteer 4 hours at the food bank, but it's super easy.
